How Verification Works

📱

1. Scan

LumeScan reads 42 signals from your vehicle's OBD-II port at 100ms intervals

🔐

2. Hash

The raw signal data is hashed with SHA-256 creating a unique fingerprint

🛡️

3. Seal

The hash is permanently sealed on the Trust Layer Ledger with a TLL Hallmark

✅

4. Verify

Anyone with the Scan ID can verify the report is authentic and unaltered
